Made to Measure

A Garment
Built for You

Our bespoke service begins with a conversation — not about trends, but about you. Who you are. How you move. What you wish to feel when you enter a room. From that conversation, we begin to construct a garment that will exist nowhere else in the world.

Every element is chosen in consultation with you: the weight of the fabric, the cut of the neckline, the fall of the hem. Nothing is standard. Nothing is approximate. Every stitch is placed with intention.

The Process

From Vision
to Creation

01
Consultation

We begin with a private consultation — in person at our Dublin atelier or by appointment elsewhere. We listen first. We sketch second. Your vision guides the entire process.

02
Measurements

Our master tailor takes over thirty individual measurements. Not sizes — measurements. The garment is engineered to your exact dimensions, your posture, your movement.

03
Creation

The garment is handmade from start to finish in our atelier. Fabric is sourced from Europe's finest mills. The construction is entirely hand-sewn. Every seam is checked, every edge finished by hand.

04
Final Fitting

Before delivery, you attend a final fitting at the atelier. Minor adjustments are made — to the hem, the seam, the moment. The garment is completed only when it is perfect.

The Atelier Experience

Where Every
Detail is Considered

Our atelier in Dublin is a quiet space — deliberate, unhurried, dedicated to the craft. When you arrive for a consultation or fitting, you are not a customer. You are a collaborator. The space is yours for the duration of your appointment.

We offer complete discretion. For clients who cannot travel to Dublin, our lead tailor is available for private appointments in select cities by arrangement.
